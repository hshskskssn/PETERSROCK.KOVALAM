Peter's Rock Homestay - Kovalam

Relax near the beach with comfort and hospitality

Where You'll Be Staying

Peter's Rock Homestay is located in Kovalam, just 13 km from Thiruvananthapuram Central Railway Station and 2 km from Lighthouse Beach. It offers free internet and has 8 rooms in a 2-storey building with car parking. Each room includes a balcony, wardrobe, and welcome items.

Guests can enjoy room service, medical assistance, a garden area, and 24-hour front desk support.

Nearby Access:
• Kovalam Junction Bus Stop – 500 m
• Trivandrum International Airport – 14 km
• Lighthouse Beach – 2 km
• Thiruvananthapuram Central Railway Station – 13 km

Top Tourist Places in Trivandrum

Kovalam Beach

Kovalam is a famous beach town known for its three nearby crescent-shaped beaches: Lighthouse Beach, Hawah Beach, and Samudra Beach. It’s a popular place for tourists because of its calm waters, great for swimming, and many fun activities to enjoy.
